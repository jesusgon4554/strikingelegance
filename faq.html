Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve all of Southern California, including Los Angeles, Inland Empire, Orange County, and surrounding areas. Delivery outside these zones is available upon request.

Is there a minimum rental order?

Yes, we require a $500 rental minimum for standard orders and $1,000 for full-service setups. This excludes delivery, taxes, and additional styling services.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks before your event date. For peak wedding season (May–October), we advise reserving 2–3 months in advance.

What’s included with full-service styling?

Full-service includes product delivery, placement, visual styling, and post-event pickup. We’ll handle the aesthetic from mood board to setup, so you can enjoy your day.

Do you offer custom packages?

Absolutely. We specialize in curated experiences — just send us your vision, guest count, and vibe, and we’ll create a tailored quote with optional upgrades.

Can I pick up the rentals myself?

For liability and protection of our inventory, we do not offer client pickups. All orders are professionally delivered and retrieved by our team.

What’s your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30+ days in advance will receive a full refund minus a 10% processing fee. Less than 30 days may result in partial or no refund depending on circumstances.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a 50% deposit is required to reserve your date and inventory. The remaining balance is due 7 days before the event.
